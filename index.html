Introducing PICKAPP Hi-GO: The Future of Electric Mobility door to door travel
ACHIEVEMENT UNLOCKED!

We are excited to announce the successful contract signings between PickApp and two esteemed partners: TODA 1 Manila Federation and Huahai Corporation in China. These significant agreements represent a major milestone in our journey to revolutionize sustainable mobility and fulfill our commitment to environmental responsibility.

Contract Signing with TODA 1 Manila Federation:

  • Partnership to introduce the cutting-edge PICKAPP HI-GO Electric Tricycle.
  • Solution for sustainable urban mobility in Manila.
  • Strategic collaboration to drive a greener future for both drivers and passengers.

Contract Signing with Huahai Corporation in China:

  • Official partnership with Huahai Corporation, a leader in electric vehicle manufacturing.
  • Huahai will be the trusted supplier of electric tricycles for PICKAPP.
  • Delivering eco-friendly vehicles with advanced technology.

Location: Ayala Technohub, Dillman Quezon City, Philippines
