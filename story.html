Our Brand Story

PICKAPP is just one of Finasiatech's many plans so that all cities and municipalities in the Philippines can continue to rise above the state and keep pace with the progress of other cities.

We all started with difficulty. We know the life of queuing and riding a tricycle… We know the feeling… We have been given the ability to make an app for Filipinos, we will do it, for fellow Filipinos!

Manuel E. Pangilinan

CEO & FOUNDER

MBA Graduate from Ateneo De Manila University with 15 years of real estate sector experience focused on retail space, office leasing and have arranged leases for over 100,000 sqm space. Crafted joint venture agreements, site acquisition and operational acquisition with total value of about PHP10 billion (c. USD200m).

Our Company's

MISSION: To uplift the life and livelihood of the tricycle drivers in the Philippines through improved transport booking technology, vehicle and ecosystem modernization, and financial inclusion of the unbanked grassroot sector of the society.

VISSION: Harnessing technology and business process to greatly contribute to growing nationwide economy on the daily transport and logistics concerns of the Filipino.
