Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2021-08-23

1. Introduction

Welcome to PICKAPP! Finasialech Corporation (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the PICKAPP service and mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when using our website and mobile app.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect data you provide to us, such as your name, contact information, and other identification details. We also collect data automatically such as location, device, and usage data to improve our services.

3. Use of Personal Data

We use your data to provide and improve our services, ensure safety, process payments, offer customer support, and comply with legal obligations. We may use anonymized data for analytics and research.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with our service providers, partners, or government authorities as required by law. We do not sell your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

This includes:

  • Insurance and financing partners
  • Service providers who perform identity verification services
  • Vehicle solutions partners, vendors, or third-party vehicle suppliers
  • Credit bureaus, other credit reporting agencies, and debt collectors

5. Cookies and Third-Party Technologies

PICKAPP and its partners use cookies and other identification technologies on our apps, websites, emails, and online ads for purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on browsers or devices by websites, apps, online media, and advertisements. PICKAPP uses cookies and similar technologies for purposes such as:

  • Authenticating users
  • Remembering user preferences and settings
  • Determining the popularity of content
  • Delivering and measuring the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Analyzing site traffic and trends
  • Understanding the online behaviors and interests of users

6. Data Retention and Deletion

PICKAPP keeps user profile, transaction, and other personal data for as long as a user maintains their PICKAPP account. We may retain or secure certain data after receiving an account deletion request if required by law.

7. Protection of Personal Data

We take reasonable legal, organizational, and technical measures to ensure that your personal data is protected. This includes measures to prevent data from being lost, misused, or accessed in an unauthorized way. However, please understand that transmission of data via the internet is not completely secure.

8. Right to Be Informed

Your personal data is treated as your personal property. It should never be collected, processed, or stored without your explicit consent, unless otherwise provided by law. PICKAPP ensures to notify you if your data has been compromised in a timely manner.
